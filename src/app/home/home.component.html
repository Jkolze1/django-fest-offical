Django Fest

If you are a woman and want to learn how to make websites, we have good news for you: we are holding a free workshop for beginners! It will take place on November 6-7 online. Kansas City Women in Technology's ongoing mission is to grow the number of women in technology careers in Kansas City.
KCWiT is excited to introduce women to coding and technology through this Django workshop. We want to give you an opportunity to learn how to program and become one of us – women programmers! This workshop is free of charge, and some meals will be provided.

FAQ

Don't see an answer to your question below? Please contact us at djangofest@kcwomenintech.org!

Do I need to know anything about websites or programming?

No! Workshops are for beginners. You don’t need to know anything about it. However, if you have a little bit of technical knowledge (i.e. you know what HTML or CSS are) you still can apply!

Do I need to have something installed on my laptop?

It would be helpful to have Django installed before workshops, but we wouldn't expect you to install anything on your own. Join us for the install party Friday night to get your laptop ready for the workshop!

What apps will I need to attend the workshop virtually?

We will be using Zoom for the event as well as Slack. Both will be free to use and we will provide instructions on getting familar with the apps if you haven't used them before!

I don't live in Kansas City, can I attend?

Of course! Groups of women from Omaha and Oklahoma City have joined us in prior years.

Is food provided?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors, lunch will be provided during workshops.
